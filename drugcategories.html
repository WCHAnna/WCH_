Drug Categories

Introduction of common drug categories =Add to favour=

Stimulants

This type drugs will speed up the body’s nervous system and create the fake energy feeling.

It make their brain very awake but when if wear off, they will fell sick and tired.

The drugs bring negative effect to them. Make they give up their life.

This type includes: Cocaine, Methamphetamines, Amphetamines, Ritalin, Cylert


Inhalants

The drugs are Sniffed or huffed and give the user immediate results

It maybe make mental damage. Also, It may make people deprived of oxygen and rapid heart beat.

It will hurt liver, lung and kidney. Besides, effect walking and feel confusion.

This type includes: Glues, Paint thinner, Gasoline, Laughing gas, Aerosol sprays,


Cannabinoids

This drug bring the feeling of euphoria and slow,

It make people confusion, memory problem, faster heart rate and anxiety.

This type includes: Hashish, Marijuana


Depressants

It will slow down the activity in your central nervous system.

However, It is be the medical to relax the body. But It will make people always feel tired.

This type includes: Barbiturates, Benzodiazepines, Flunitrazepam, GHB (Gamma-hydroxybutyrate), Methaqualone, Alcohol, Tranquillisers,


Opioids and Morphine Derivatives

It may make people derivatives can cause drowsiness, confusion, nausea, feelings of euphoria, respiratory complications and relieve pain.

This type includes: Codeine, Fentanyl and fentanyl analogs, Heroin, Morphine, Opium, Oxycodone HCL, Hydrocodone bitartrate, acetaminophen


Anabolic Steroids

This drugs will streng the muscles and physical performance.

Also it brings baldness, cysts, oily hair and skin, acne, heart attack, stroke and change in voice. Hostility is also a frequent side effect of anabolic steroids.

This type includes: Anadrol, Durabolin, Oxandrin, Stanozol, Dianabol,


Hallucinogens

This drugs can change emotion frequently and appear illusion.

Also, it effect people control their body, speech and hostility.

Besides, It raise the heartbeat, higher blood pressure, heart failure.

This type includes: Psilocybin, Mescaline, LSD (lysergic acid diethylamide), Magic Mushrooms,


Prescription Drugs

The prescription drugs are legal and helpful by guidance og doctor.

But if it is abused, it will be very dangerous to health.

Opiods: Codeine, Oxycodone, Morphine, Central nervous system depressants: barbiturates, benzodiazepines, Stimulants: dextroamphetamine, methylphenidate
